ARPANET:
1. It stands for Advanced Research Project Agency Network.
2. It is the first ever computer network developed.
3. The first permanent ARPANET link was established on 21 November 1969, between the IMP at UCLA and the IMP at the Stanford Research Institute.
4. It uses packet-switching technique to transmit data. Packet-switching means that bits of information can take virtually any route and still end up at its final destination.
